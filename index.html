SOUTH · LIFE & THINKING

Things I think about

I’m a designer who likes to make the thing, not just describe it. I draw, I prototype in code, I build small games on the side. I think about design in my spare time.

I love systems thinking — how design and systems come together to solve real problems. Architecture and city planning pull at me for the same reason.

I came up through video games and mobile, did a stint in consulting and advertising, and ended up in tech designing system-level interfaces.

Outside of work: urban sketching, my dog, and my son Theo.
