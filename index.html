Maker. Designer. Builder.

based · seattle

I'm an experience designer, and curiosity has been the through-line across fifteen years of wide-ranging work. Interfaces for story-driven games, dashboards for government agencies, tools for game developers, and now gaming experiences for players all over the world. The domains keep shifting. The appetite for what's next never does.

I grew up making art and never stopped. I love experimenting with different mediums and figuring out what each one can do. AI has given me a new one: computers as the medium itself. I'm still exploring what's possible. I do my best work in small teams where the ownership is real and the problems are novel.
